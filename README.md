# Approvr

Approvr is a premium B2B SaaS focused on the **Approval Loop** for agencies, studios, and independent creatives.

### The Core Promise
Approvr simplifies the path from **Deliverable Sent** to **Final Approval**, replacing fragmented email threads and Slack messages with one high-end branded portal for your clients.

### Strategic Priorities
- **Premium Aesthetics**: Maintaining an "editorial-grade" visual experience.
- **Low Interface Energy**: Minimizing cognitive load for external clients.
- **High-Velocity Frictionless Sign-off**: Eliminating everything that blocks a simple "Approve" button.

### Strategic Non-Goals (The "Compliance Swamp")
Approvr is explicitly **not** a legal-tech or enterprise compliance platform. We will never delve into:
- **E-signatures or DocuSign-style legal compliance**.
- **Audit trails for regulated industries (FDA, Medical, etc.)**.
- **Project Management bloat** (No invoicing, no internal chat, no tasks, no billing).

By staying out of the "Compliance Swamp," Approvr remains the leanest, most elegant choice for creative agencies who just want their work signed off.
